FISH STEW RECIPE

Recipe description

Ugandan fish stew is one of the finest stews, i have ever tateste in lif. it is delicius thick andsuper yummy.

Ingredients

  • Flesh or smoked fish
  • clean water
  • onions
  • salt
  • tomatoes
  • greenpaper
  • olive oil
  • mixed spices

procedure

  1. chop the fish into small enough pieces
  2. Then cut the greenpaper, onions, tomatoes and garlic on a chopping board and put them separate
  3. Heat the pan for about 5 minutes, add olive oil and then drop in the onions, let them cook until gold in colur and add the garlic
  4. Add all other ingreadients at once plus little amount of water, cover and leave it to cook for around 30 minuts incase using a pressure cooker
  5. After the stew is well cooked our stew is ready to be served
